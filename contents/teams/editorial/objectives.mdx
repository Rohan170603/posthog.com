## Mini newsletters & blogs to explain trending dev topics

**Owner:** <TeamMember name="Jina Yoon" photo />

**Motivation:** Ian's loops article [did really well on Twitter](https://posthog.slack.com/archives/C09GU689J1X/p1782295022075899?thread_ts=1782289510.165929&cid=C09GU689J1X) and we're getting feedback that it's valuable to have simple explainers for overly-hyped and over-complicated topics. 

**What we'll ship:** A mini-newsletter or blog every two weeks explaining a trending topic, buzzword, or concept in simple terms. Aim for 2-minute reads with newsletter-level quality, written with the X and YC audience in mind. Example topics: agents, skills, orchestration. 

**We'll know we're successful when:** They perform well on Twitter and people are referencing them as a great explainer on that topic. SEO/AEO will likely benefit but that's not a primary goal.
