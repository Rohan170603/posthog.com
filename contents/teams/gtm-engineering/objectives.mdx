### Q3 2026 Objectives

### **Lead Gateway** - (Driver: <TeamMember name="Abhischek Thottakara" />)

Build a single source for lead tasks that get routed to Sales Teams to consistently enrich and score product led signals in order to improve qualification and win rates.

**What we'll ship:**

* REST Webhook endpoint to receive product led / inbound signals
* Set of temporal workflows to enrich the person and company consistently and persist the task in Salesforce
* Scoring engine to rank Account potential and propensity for the lead
* Migrate all existing lead triggers from Zapier, Default and Vitally into a set of SQL based trigger definitions
* Implement CRON based revival of leads based on growth signals updated scores

### **posthog-salesforce** - (Driver: <TeamMember name="Abhischek Thottakara" />)

Deploy [posthog-salesforce](https://github.com/posthog/posthog-salesforce) and make it the source of truth for task routing, account and opportunity management to improve data quality and enablement for GTM Humans to propose changes.

**What we'll ship:**

* Deploy posthog-salesforce to production
* Lock down Salesforce to avoid manual changes to production
