# ClickHouse at PostHog - Mission

The ClickHouse team's mission is to provide a central data store for PostHog that is **reliable**, **fast**, **cost efficient**, and **secure**.

We run PostHog's self-managed, multi-petabyte ClickHouse fleet.

We provide a platform that is:

- **Reliable** - replicated, durable storage with backups and regularly tested restores, workloads isolated to limit the blast radius of failures, and upgrades and operations that don't cause downtime
- **Fast** - each workload runs on the right size and class of nodes, with data intelligently tiered across storage
- **Cost efficient** - data stored compactly and compressed, with a cluster topology that keeps cost in check as we grow
- **Secure** - access tightly controlled, secrets short-lived and easily rotated, and data protected in transit and at rest
